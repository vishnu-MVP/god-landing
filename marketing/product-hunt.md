# Product Hunt Launch

**Product Name:** GOD — Omniscient Prediction Markets

**Tagline:** See tomorrow, today. The prediction market powered by an autonomous ML oracle.

**Description:**

GOD is a prediction market platform that watches the world in real time — 100+ live data streams across news, markets, social signals, weather, and geopolitics — and forecasts what happens next with calibrated, explainable probabilities.

Unlike traditional prediction markets that rely on crowd wisdom, GOD uses an autonomous ML engine that:
- Monitors the world 24/7 across 6 event categories
- Connects non-obvious causal chains across domains
- Produces calibrated probability scores for every forecast
- Shows you the reasoning and evidence behind every prediction
- Tracks its own accuracy and improves over time

**What you get:**
- 🔮 Prophecies — forecasted events with confidence scores, causal chains, and supporting evidence
- 🌐 Globe View — predictions geolocated on an interactive 3D globe
- 📊 Markets — Polymarket-style prediction markets across Politics, Geopolitics, Economy, Crypto, Technology, and Climate
- 📈 Accuracy Tracking — every prediction scored for accuracy, with transparent performance metrics
- 🏆 Leaderboard — compete on calibration, not just luck

**Pricing:**
- Seeker (Free): browse markets, view predictions, globe, feed, leaderboard
- Oracle ($29/mo): dark signal alerts, causal chains, watchlists, early market access
- Prophet ($99/mo): full API access, raw probabilities, webhooks, historical archive

**Maker Comment (to post at launch):**
