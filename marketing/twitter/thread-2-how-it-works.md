1/ People keep asking: how does GOD actually predict the future?

It's not magic. It's not a single model. It's a pipeline that watches, connects, and forecasts.

Here's how it works (without giving away the secret sauce) 🧵

2/ Step 1: WATCH THE WORLD
GOD continuously ingests 100+ live data streams — news, markets, social signals, weather, seismic activity, satellite data, and more.

24/7 real-time monitoring. No human could track this manually.

3/ Step 2: CONNECT THE DOTS
The engine finds non-obvious connections between events across domains.

A policy change in one country → trade flow shift → market impact → downstream event. The engine traces these causal chains automatically.

4/ Step 3: FORECAST WITH CONFIDENCE
Every prediction comes with a calibrated probability score. "70% likely" means it actually happens ~70% of the time — not just a raw model output.

Calibration > raw accuracy. This is the key insight.

5/ Step 4: SHOW YOUR WORK
Every prediction includes:
- The reasoning trail
- The causal chain
- The supporting evidence
- The confidence history (how it evolved over time)

You can verify WHY the engine made a prediction. No black box.

6/ Step 5: TRACK & IMPROVE
Every prediction is scored against the actual outcome. The system learns from its hits and misses and adapts over time.

Accuracy is transparent — you can see exactly how often GOD is right.

7/ The result: a prediction market where the forecasts come from an autonomous engine, not the crowd.
