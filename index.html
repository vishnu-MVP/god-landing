How It Works

An 8-layer engine that watches, thinks, and predicts.

From raw data ingestion to calibrated probabilistic forecasts — every prediction is backed by a traceable causal chain and real evidence.

L1 → L2

Ingest & Stream

177+ collectors across 12 domains feed Redis Streams — dedup, enrich, and route in real time.

L3 → L4

Extract & Graph

Gemini LLM extracts structured events. Neo4j builds a temporal knowledge graph of entities, events, and causal edges.

L5 → L6

Dark Signals & Correlation

Granger causality, transfer entropy, and mutual information discover non-obvious cause→effect chains. Dark intelligence detects absences and behavioral shifts.

L7 → L8

Predict & Display

Bayesian + Hawkes + Transformer ensemble produces calibrated probabilities. Platt-scaled, Brier-scored, served through a real-time globe UI.
